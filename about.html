About Us

At EverVenturez, we have the best selection of rides you'll ever see! We have everything from a Mystical Corner to an amazing dining hall with great food! We have roller-coasters,bumper cars and a lot more including a Unicorn Car! This will be the greatest experience of your life! Come on Friday and you will see beautiful fireworks! We have over 50 mystical attractions, and plenty of options for fine dining! We have a small section for kids under 8 years. You can click here to see our amazing little kids corner!

If you don't want to eat at our dining, we have lots of snack posts, food trucks, and even a dining hall, all serving lots of delicious foods and snacks for affordable prices! We have an amazing The Hulk that is one of our most popular attractions!
